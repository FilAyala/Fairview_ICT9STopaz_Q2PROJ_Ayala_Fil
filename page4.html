Yema Cake

Yema Cake with custard frosting and shredded cheese is soft, fluffy, and flavorful. This Filipino sponge cake is the perfect dessert for anytime you need a special sweet treat.

Halo Halo

Halo-halo is a Filipino-style shaved ice made with sweetened beans, fruits, and jellies and topped with milk, Leche flan, purple yam jam, and ice cream. It's a cold and refreshing treat.

Leche Flan

Leche Flan is the ultimate sweet treat! This classic Filipino custard has a rich, smooth, and creamy texture with a golden caramel topping making it even more delicious.
