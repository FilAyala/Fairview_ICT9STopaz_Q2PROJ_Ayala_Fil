Description

Sinantomas

Sinantomas is made of tasty pork ribs and potatoes braised in soy sauce, pineapple juice, ketchup, and cheese. Full of sweet and savory flavors you'll love as an appetizer or as a main dish.

Pork Adobo

Pork Adobo is made with pork belly braised in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and onions. A balance of salty and savory, this hearty stew is the Philippines' national dish!

Sinigang na Baboy

Sinigang na Baboy is made with pork ribs, vegetables, and tamarind-flavored broth, it's hearty and delicious on its own or served with steamed rice.
