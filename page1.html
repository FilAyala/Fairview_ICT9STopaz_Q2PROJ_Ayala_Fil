Longganisa

Longganisa is a staple Filipino breakfast . This savory-sweet sausage is made by stuffing hog-casing with ground pork, deliciously flavored with spices and seasonings.

Champorado

Champorado is the ultimate breakfast! This Filipino rice porridge is easy to make with only five ingredients and is delicious and comforting with a very nice chocolate flavor. Both kids and adults love it!

Pandesal

Pandesal is a popular bread in the Philippines! With a golden, crumb-coated exterior, slightly sweet taste, soft and fluffy texture. This Filipino bread is delicious on its own or with your choice of filling. Perfect for breakfast or as a snack!
